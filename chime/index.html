Redmi 9T/POCO M3

Device summary


Redmi 9T and POCO M3 is an entry-level smartphone by Xiaomi released on February 2021. These two devices based on Qualcomm's Snapdragon 662 (SM6115). Because of so many hardware and software similarity, Redmi 9T(lime) and POCO M3(citrus) being unified as Chime.

Redmi 9T has several other variants like Redmi Power(lime), Redmi Note 9 4G China(lime), Redmi 9T NFC(lemon), and Redmi 9T South America (pomelo).
